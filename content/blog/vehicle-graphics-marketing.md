# How Vehicle Graphics Help Local Service Businesses Get Noticed

A service vehicle is already moving through the neighbourhoods a business wants to reach. Lettering, decals and wraps can turn that movement into steady visibility.

The goal is not to cover every inch with information. The goal is to make the business name, service and contact path easy to understand quickly.

## Start with distance

People often see a vehicle while walking, driving or looking out a window. That means the design has to work from a distance before it works up close.

Focus on the business name, core service, phone or website, strong contrast and a simple layout.

## Choose the right coverage

Vehicle lettering can be enough for a clean professional look. A partial wrap adds more colour and shape. A full wrap can carry a larger campaign or stronger brand presence.

## Plan for the actual vehicle

Vehicle year, make, model, colour, door handles, curves and windows affect the layout. Photos and measurements should be reviewed before final artwork is produced.

## Connect it with the rest of the business

The same campaign can continue through uniforms, signs, business cards, landing pages and direct mail. Consistent wording helps people recognize the business again later.
