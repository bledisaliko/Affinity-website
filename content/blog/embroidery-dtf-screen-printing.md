# Embroidery, DTF or Screen Printing? How to Choose for Your Apparel Order

The same logo can look completely different depending on how it is applied to the garment.

Embroidery adds thread and texture. DTF can reproduce detailed full-colour artwork. Screen printing creates bold colour directly on the garment. None of these methods is automatically the best choice for every order.

## When embroidery makes sense

Embroidery is a strong choice for polos, jackets, work shirts, hats, beanies, toques, uniforms and corporate apparel.

Thread gives the logo a dimensional professional finish. Very small text, thin lines and gradients may need to be simplified.

## When DTF printing makes sense

DTF printing is useful for full-colour logos, detailed artwork, gradients, T-shirts, hoodies, smaller mixed orders and artwork with several colours.

## When screen printing makes sense

Screen printing is often a good fit for larger quantities, team shirts, events, repeat uniforms, bold designs and limited-colour artwork.
