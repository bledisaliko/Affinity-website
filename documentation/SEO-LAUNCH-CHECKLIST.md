# Affinity Creative SEO Launch Checklist

These items happen after the generated site is uploaded to `affinitycreative.ca`. They require real account access, real business details or ongoing marketing work.

## Google Search Console

- Verify the `affinitycreative.ca` domain property.
- Submit `https://affinitycreative.ca/sitemap.xml`.
- Inspect the homepage.
- Inspect `/embroidery/`.
- Inspect `/vinyl-graphics/`.
- Request indexing after launch or major updates.
- Monitor Page Indexing for crawl, duplicate, redirect or soft-404 issues.
- Monitor Core Web Vitals on mobile and desktop.
- Review search queries monthly and improve pages using real query data.
- Keep title, description and FAQ changes grounded in what customers actually search and ask.

## Google Business Profile

- Use the exact business name: Affinity Creative.
- Use the exact phone: 437-313-1510.
- Use the exact website: `https://affinitycreative.ca`.
- Choose the most accurate primary category for the business.
- Add relevant secondary categories only when they honestly fit.
- Add real services, including custom embroidery, apparel printing, business printing, signs, vinyl graphics and digital marketing.
- Add real project and production photos when available.
- Keep the service area accurate for Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area.
- Create a genuine customer-review request process.
- Respond to reviews.
- Do not purchase reviews.
- Do not add fake locations.
- Do not create duplicate profiles.

Local visibility is influenced by relevance, distance and prominence. Website improvements can strengthen relevance and conversion, but they cannot override distance by themselves.

## Citations and Consistency

Keep the same business details wherever the business is listed:

- Google Business Profile
- Bing Places
- Apple Business Connect
- Social profiles
- Relevant local directories
- Supplier or industry directories
- Real association memberships

Avoid spam directory submissions and any listing that requires inaccurate categories, fake locations or low-quality reciprocal links.

## Backlinks and Authority

Legitimate opportunities to pursue:

- Supplier listings where Affinity Creative is an actual customer or partner.
- Business partner pages where the relationship is real.
- Customer project features with permission.
- Local chambers and business organizations.
- Community sponsorship pages.
- Industry associations.
- Useful original articles that answer real customer questions.
- Real project case studies with photos, constraints and outcomes.

Avoid:

- Bulk backlink packages.
- Private blog networks.
- Fake guest posts.
- Review manipulation.
- Doorway city pages without unique local value.

## Content After Launch

- Replace generic imagery with real project photos as soon as available.
- Rename new production images descriptively without keyword stuffing.
- Add real captions when they help users understand materials or decisions.
- Use Search Console data to identify which services need more practical detail.
- Add project case studies only when the business can show real work and permission is clear.

## Technical Checks After Upload

- Confirm `https://affinitycreative.ca/robots.txt` loads and references the sitemap.
- Confirm `https://affinitycreative.ca/sitemap.xml` includes the public routes only.
- Inspect `/embroidery/` and `/vinyl-graphics/` on mobile.
- Submit a test quote only if the PHP endpoint is configured on the live host.
- Check that phone and email links work on a real device.
- Run a fresh Lighthouse test against the live site, not only local preview.
