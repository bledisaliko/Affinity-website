## Content Rules

- Write for customers first and use search terms only where they fit the section.
- Do not create thin city pages unless real, unique local information is supplied.
- Do not add a meta keywords tag.
- Do not claim guaranteed rankings.
- Use Search Console data after launch to refine headings, FAQs and internal links.
